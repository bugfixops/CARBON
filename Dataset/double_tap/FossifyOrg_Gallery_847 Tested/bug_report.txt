Invalid "fill screen" zoom for GIF images on double-tap

App: FossifyOrg/Gallery
Version: 1.10.0
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Gallery/issues/847
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Gallery/releases/download/1.10.0/gallery-24-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.10.0
Gestures: zoom, enlarge, double-tap, tap

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Open any GIF image
  2. Double-tap it

Expected Behavior:
  The GIF is zoomed in to fill the screen, similar to how double-tap works for still images.

Actual Behavior:
  Depending on its size, the GIF file is either too enlarged or not enlarged enough.
