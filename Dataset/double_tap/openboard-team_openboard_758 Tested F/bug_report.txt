Accessibility: The button to go to the previous level does not work properly

App: openboard-team/openboard
Version: 1.4.5
Issue: https://github.com/openboard-team/openboard/issues/758
APK: https://github.com/openboard-team/openboard/releases/download/v1.4.5/app-release.apk
APK Tag: v1.4.5
Primary Gesture: double-tap

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Install a screen reader (TalkBack or Jieshuo) on your device. Then - turn on the TalkBack or Jieshuo service in the accessibility settings;
  2. open Open Board settings;
  3. Go to any settings section;
  4. Double-tap the button in the upper left corner of the screen to go back one level. Actual result: nothing happens. Expected result: after activating this button, the focus should be at the previous level. Note: By default, screen readers are configured to activate an element with a double tap. This is convenient for most blind users. Open Board version: 1.4.5 Device: Impression ImSmart-C551 (Android 6.0). I would be very grateful for your attention to this request.
