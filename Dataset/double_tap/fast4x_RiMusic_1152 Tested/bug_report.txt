### Steps to reproduce the bug

1. Open the player view.

2. Tap once on the song title or artist name.

### Expected behavior

• Single tapping on the song title or artist name should redirect to the respective page.

• Tapping on the artist name should lead to the artist page, not to the song tab.

### Actual behavior

• Requires double tapping to be redirected.

https://github.com/fast4x/RiMusic/assets/71184627/9616b3b7-189c-423e-a8a2-7507dc822d3c

• Tapping on the artist name opens the song tab instead of the artist page.

### RiMusic version

0.6.26.1

### Android version

Android 13

### Additional information

The issue I have with this is twofold. Firstly, the need for double tapping creates a perception of unresponsiveness and diminishes the user experience. Secondly, when tapping on the artist name, instead of being directed to the artist page, the song tab is opened, which is confusing and counterintuitive. Improving the linking behavior to require only a single tap and ensuring that tapping on the artist name leads to the artist page would significantly enhance usability and user satisfaction.
