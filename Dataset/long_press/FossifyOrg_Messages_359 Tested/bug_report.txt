Can't scroll or see participants on conversation details page

App: FossifyOrg/Messages
Version: 1.1.6
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Messages/issues/359
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Messages/releases/download/1.1.6/messages-9-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.1.6
Gestures: long press, scroll, orientation, landscape, tap, press

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Long press on any conversation
  2. Tap **More options**
  3. Tap **Conversation details**
  4. Switch to landscape mode (not necessary if it's group with many participants)
  5. Try to scroll to view participants

Expected Behavior:
  The list should scroll. 

One should be able to view all the information on the page regardless of device orientation.

Actual Behavior:
  The list does not scroll.

Displayed information is limited by screen height.
