Accidently creating invisible folders in dock

App: FossifyOrg/Launcher
Version: 1.6.0
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Launcher/issues/304
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Launcher/releases/download/1.6.0/launcher-12-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.6.0
Gestures: drag and drop

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Have a app on the bottom right most location on your app dock at home screen.
  2. Drag and drop another app or same app onto the bottom right most location where you should already have a app there.
  3. Invisible folder is created and takes up that space.
  4. Drag and attempt to drop another app to that bugged location where the invisible folder is located on your dock at home screen.
  5. Quickly move the wanted app during attempt to a non-dock location at home screen.
  6. Ensure the invisible folder appears and stays open. (If the invisible folder does not stay open in order for one to remove the existing app in it, redo fix steps 1 & 2.)
  7. Touch and hold app in the invisible folder and remove it.
  8. Ensure that no more apps are in this invisible folder by attempting fix steps 1 & 2 again or if the invisible folder is still open then remove the remaining apps in it.
  9. Once all apps are removed from the invisible folder in the dock at home screen, one should now be able to successfully place a app in that location on the dock at home screen without issue.

Expected Behavior:
  A folder should not be able to be created in the dock at home screen as this is not a feature yet.  One should be able to manipulate the apps in this folder by click on the folder in attempt to manipulate the apps within it.  Please feel free to correct me if I am wrong.

Actual Behavior:
  Invisible folder is created and takes up that space.  One can add more and more apps into this invisible folder.  One can not access the invisible folder without attempt to add more apps to this invisible folder.
