### Describe the bug

Any attemp to add any widget results in a LawnChair crash, and no widget added.

### Steps to reproduce

Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Open "widgets" tab
2. Select any widget
3. Drag and drop it to the home screen
4. Try to add it

### Expected behavior

No crash, obviously, and a working widget.
