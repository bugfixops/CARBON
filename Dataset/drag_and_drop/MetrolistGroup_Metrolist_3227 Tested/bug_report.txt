Replacement of new song with old song

App: MetrolistGroup/Metrolist
Version: 13.3.0
Issue: https://github.com/MetrolistGroup/Metrolist/issues/3227
APK: https://github.com/MetrolistGroup/Metrolist/releases/download/v13.3.0/Metrolist-with-Google-Cast.apk
APK Tag: v13.3.0
Gestures: drag the

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Add a song to a playlist in custom orde
  2. Drag the new song at the bottom to the top of the playlist

Expected Behavior:
  The new song should be dragged to the top with no problems

Actual Behavior:
  The new song will be replaced with the old one when the new song is dragged to the top
