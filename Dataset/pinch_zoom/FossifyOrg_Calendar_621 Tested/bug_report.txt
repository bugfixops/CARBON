Zoom level in weekly view locks

App: FossifyOrg/Calendar
Version: 1.3.0
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Calendar/issues/621
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Calendar/releases/download/1.3.0/calendar-7-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.3.0
Gestures: zoom in, zoom out, zoom, swipe, swipe right

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Go to the Weekly view
  2. Zoom out as far as you can vertically
  3. Swipe right to get to the next week
  4. Observe that you can no longer zoom in vertically

Expected Behavior:
  I can zoom vertically.

Actual Behavior:
  I cannot zoom vertically. Restarting the app doesn't fix it, only erasing all app data.
