(Deep zooming) Can not pan after releasing only one finger after pinch zooming

App: FossifyOrg/Gallery
Version: 1.8.0
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Gallery/issues/728
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Gallery/releases/download/1.8.0/gallery-20-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.8.0
Gestures: pinch, pinch to zoom, pinch zoom, zoom in, zoom, two finger, touch
Limitation: Cat A: Pointer count transition (2->1->2). Cannot lift one finger while keeping another down within a single touch session. uiautomator2 atomic touch sessions. SELinux blocks raw sendevent.

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. "Allow deep zooming images" must be enabled
  2. Open a picture
  3. With two fingers, (un)pinch to zoom in
  4. Release only one finger and try to pan with the other
  5. Place the finger back and try to zoom in/out or pan

Expected Behavior:
  According to touch input:
At step 4, the image should be panned.
At step 5, the image should be zoomed or panned.

Actual Behavior:
  After step 3, when one finger is released, no further pan/zoom happens despite user input:
At step 4, the image is not panned at all.
At step 5, the image is not zoomed nor panned at all.
