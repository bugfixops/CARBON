**Describe the bug**
After rotation, the Privacy Notice button disappears. In landscape mode, scrolling is not possible, so it cannot be found by scrolling.

**To Reproduce**
Steps to reproduce the behavior:
1. Rotate to landscape mode.
2. See error. The **Privacy Notice** button disappears, and scrolling is not possible.

**Expected behavior**
The Privacy Notice button should remain visible after rotation, or it should be accessible by scrolling.
