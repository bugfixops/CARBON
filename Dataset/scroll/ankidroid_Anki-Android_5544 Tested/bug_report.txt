AnkiDroid scroll bug

App: ankidroid/Anki-Android
Version: 2.8.4
Issue: https://github.com/ankidroid/Anki-Android/issues/5544
APK: 
APK Tag: v2.8.4
Primary Gesture: none_scroll
Keywords: scroll; swipe_region


Steps to reproduce: Scale card browser to 110% (instead of default 100%).
Result: The font size is much to large.
Ankidroid versions: 2.9.1 and before

Workaround: use a much smaller scaling, e. g. 30 or 40% to be able to scale in minor steps and to achieve the desired font scaling. Of course this workaround is not obvious to new users who will try 110% first.

BTW On my mobile phone 100% font scaling seems to correspond to a scaling of 35% (which cannot be set).
