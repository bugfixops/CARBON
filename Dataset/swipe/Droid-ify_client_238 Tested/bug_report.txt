**Describe the bug**
The app crashes every time any option in the settings section is altered. The crash occurs on back navigation.

**To Reproduce**
Steps to reproduce the behavior:

1. Open the app and go to the settings section.
2. Click on any settings toggle to alter it. I faced this issue for the notify new versions, material you theme and unstable updates options.
3. Go back to previous screen either using the back arrow on the top bar or gesture navigation.
4. The app will crash.

**Expected behavior**
Returning to the previous screen is the expected behaviour. This does not happen, rather the app crashes.
