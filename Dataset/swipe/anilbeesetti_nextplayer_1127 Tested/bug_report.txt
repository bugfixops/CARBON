Vertical swipe misbehaviour — volume/brightness gesture too sensitive in landscape

App: anilbeesetti/nextplayer
Version: 0.12.3
Issue: https://github.com/anilbeesetti/nextplayer/issues/1127
APK: https://github.com/anilbeesetti/nextplayer/releases/download/v0.16.3/nextplayer-v0.16.3-arm64-v8a-release.apk
APK Tag: v0.16.3
Gestures: swipe, volume gesture, brightness gesture

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Open a video in landscape mode
  2. Try to swipe down from the top to open the notification drawer
  3. Observe that the volume or brightness changes instead

Expected Behavior:
  The swipe gesture should not be overly sensitive. It should work the way other players' swipe feature works, allowing the notification drawer to be pulled down without triggering volume/brightness changes.

Actual Behavior:
  The vertical swipe feature for volume and brightness in landscape mode is too sensitive. Whenever trying to swipe down the notification drawer, it reduces volume or brightness depending on which side of the screen the swipe started from.

--- Full Issue Body ---
The vertical swipe feature for volume and brightness when in landscape mode are too much sensitive whenever I try to swipe down my top bar/Notification drawer it reduces my volume or brightness depending upon which side of the screen I had swiped from.
