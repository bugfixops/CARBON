Accessibility - have screen reader anounce existence/count of events on a day in monthly view

App: FossifyOrg/Calendar
Version: 1.10.3
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Calendar/issues/1103
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Calendar/releases/download/1.10.3/calendar-20-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.10.3
Primary Gesture: talkback

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Enable the TalkBack screen reader.
  2. Open the calendar app and navigate to the monthly view.
  3. Move through the days of the month using TalkBack navigation.
  4. Focus on days that visually contain events (these days are highlighted in the UI).

Expected Behavior:
  When focusing a day in monthly view with TalkBack, the screen reader should announce that the day contains events. Ideally, it should also announce the number of events, or at least indicate that events exist on that day, similar to the visual highlight provided in the UI.

Actual Behavior:
  TalkBack treats days with events the same as days without events. No information about the presence of events is announced, so users relying on TalkBack cannot efficiently identify which days contain events in the monthly view.
