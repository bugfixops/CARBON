Pinch-in zoom breaks player gestures — volume and brightness become unresponsive

App: A-EDev/Flow
Version: 2.0.5
Issue: https://github.com/A-EDev/Flow/issues/284
APK: https://github.com/A-EDev/Flow/releases/download/v2.0.5/flow-foss.apk
APK Tag: v2.0.5
Gestures: pinch, swipe, volume gesture, brightness gesture

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Open Flow and navigate to any video
  2. Start playback
  3. Perform a pinch-to-zoom gesture on the video (zoom the video itself)
  4. Try to use swipe gestures to change volume or brightness

Expected Behavior:
  The system gestures for volume and brightness should function normally regardless of the video player's zoom state.

Actual Behavior:
  When the video player is zoomed, the volume and brightness gestures are completely ignored. No volume change or brightness adjustment occurs, even when the gesture is performed. 100% reproducible.

--- Full Issue Body ---
It appears to be a conflict between the video player's zoom functionality and the system gesture overlays. When using the video player in Flow and attempting to pinch-zoom the video, the system gestures for controlling Volume and Brightness become unresponsive.
