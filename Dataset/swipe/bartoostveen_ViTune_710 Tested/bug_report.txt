Notification shows wrong album art for current song
App: 25huizengek1/ViTune
Version: 0.12.3
Issue: https://github.com/25huizengek1/ViTune/issues/710
Gestures: swipe, notification

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Play a song in ViTune
  2. Swipe down to open notification panel
  3. Observe the notification shows wrong album art

Expected Behavior:
  Notification should show correct album art for the currently playing song.

Actual Behavior:
  Notification displays incorrect album art that doesn't match the current song.
