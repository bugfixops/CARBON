Fullscreen gesture conflict — brightness/volume gestures trigger when opening control panel

App: A-EDev/Flow
Version: 1.3.4
Issue: https://github.com/A-EDev/Flow/issues/27
APK: https://github.com/A-EDev/Flow/releases/download/v2.0.5/flow-foss.apk
APK Tag: v2.0.5
Gestures: swipe, volume gesture, brightness gesture

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Play a video in fullscreen mode
  2. Try to open the control panel (swipe down from top for notifications)

Expected Behavior:
  The notification/control panel should open without triggering brightness or volume gestures.

Actual Behavior:
  When trying to use the control panel to see notifications while in fullscreen mode, the gestures for brightness and volume get triggered instead. The gesture sensitivity is too high and doesn't distinguish between a notification panel pull and a media control swipe.

--- Full Issue Body ---
When you want to use the control panel to see your notifications, etc., and you are in full-screen mode, the gestures for brightness and volume get triggered. It might be a good idea to check the sensitivity of these gestures.
