--- Full Issue Body ---
### Steps to reproduce

1. Open the app and start playback (video mode) of any item.
2. While playback is active, press the Android Back button once to minimize the player.
3. Observe the minimize animation.
4. Repeat the same action but minimize the player using the in-player swipe/gesture instead of Back.
5. Repeat steps 1–4 in Audio mode.

### Expected behavior

- Minimizing the player should animate smoothly regardless of how the action is triggered. The Back button and the swipe/gesture should produce identical, fluid minimize animations in both Video and Audio modes.

### Actual behavior

- When minimizing via the Back button, the minimize animation is (especially in its beginning) less smooth compared with the gesture-triggered minimize, which is perfectly fluid. This occurs in both Video and Audio playback modes. The visual lag is consistent and reproducible.

### LibreTube version

0.30.0

### Android version

Android 16

- I know this is a minor issue but it affects UX polish.
