### Steps to reproduce the bug

1. Switch the navigation bar to "Swipe gestures" in your device's system settings (leave it on "Swipe from sides and bottom")
2. Open Metrolist
3. Play a song and click the mini-player to open the (non-mini) player
4. Swipe from the side for the back gesture
5. Notice how it doesn't work even after repeated back gesture swipes

### Expected behavior

I expected the player to exit to the mini-player when I initiated the back gesture.

### Actual behavior

Instead, using the back gesture doesn't do anything.
