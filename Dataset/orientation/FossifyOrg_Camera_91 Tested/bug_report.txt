Countdown timer does not honor device orientation

App: FossifyOrg/Camera
Version: 1.0.2
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Camera/issues/91
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Camera/releases/download/1.0.2/camera-3-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.0.2
Gestures: rotate, rotation, orientation, landscape, portrait
Primary Gesture: orientation

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. 0.2 (All versions are affected)
  2. Open the app
  3. Set the timer to 10 seconds
  4. Tap the shutter button
  5. Change device orientation to landscape

Expected Behavior:
  Countdown text rotates on device rotation, just like the buttons in the top bar.

Actual Behavior:
  Countdown text is locked in portrait mode.
