The current selected day, month, week, year is not preserved after rotating

App: FossifyOrg/Calendar
Version: 1.10.2
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Calendar/issues/1042
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Calendar/releases/download/1.10.2/calendar-19-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.10.2
Gestures: rotate
Primary Gesture: orientation

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Go to the day view
  2. Swipe or tap to the next day
  3. Rotate the device

Expected Behavior:
  I expect to see the same day I saw before rotating

Actual Behavior:
  The day which was used to load the view is shown instead.
