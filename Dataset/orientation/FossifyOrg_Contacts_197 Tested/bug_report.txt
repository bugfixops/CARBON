View always changed to contact list when rotating the phone

App: FossifyOrg/Contacts
Version: 1.1.0
Issue: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Contacts/issues/197
APK: https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Contacts/releases/download/1.1.0/contacts-3-foss-release.apk
APK Tag: 1.1.0
Gestures: rotate, landscape, portrait
Primary Gesture: orientation

Steps to Reproduce:
  1. Open the app
  2. Select either of ‘favourites’ or ‘groups’ tab, if not already selected
  3. Rotate the phone by 90 degrees, so that the view goes from portrait to landscape, or viceversa

Expected Behavior:
  The selected tab should remain active in the new view

Actual Behavior:
  The ‘contact list’ tab is always shown
